Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
- Always verify the sender before sharing personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone
- Check website URLs carefully before entering login details

UPI Safety Tips:
- Do not approve unknown payment requests in UPI apps
- Always verify the receiver’s details before sending money

Reporting:
- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline number: 1930
